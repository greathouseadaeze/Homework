All About Natural Hair

Do you have natural hair but not sure how to care for it? Worry no more! I am here to help and provide some helpful information on how to care for your natural hair! Lets get started!

Best Tools For Natural Hair

  1. Wide-tooth Detangling Comb
  2. Denman Brush
  3. Alligator Hair Clips
  4. Bonnet Hair Dryer
  5. Spray/Misting Bottle
  6. No Snag Hair Ties
  7. Silk Scarfs

Styling Tips

  1. Always detangle hair starting from ends of hair to roots.
  2. Use a conditoner with a lot of slip.
  3. Always use low heat when styling not to cause heat damage to curls.
  4. Use a heat protectant when flat ironing hair.
  5. Do not wear hair styles with a lot of tension.
